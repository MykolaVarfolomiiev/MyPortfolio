About Me

Precision Meets Innovation

I'm a highly motivated professional making a strategic transition from nuclear power plant operations to software development. My journey began in Ukraine, where I spent over 11 years operating complex systems at the Rivne Nuclear Power Plant, monitoring critical performance data, and ensuring zero safety incidents.

Now based in Moncton, New Brunswick, I'm channeling that same analytical mindset and attention to detail into building software. Currently pursuing my Information Technology diploma at New Brunswick Community College, I'm developing expertise in C#, Java, ASP.NET, and modern web technologies.

What sets me apart is my unique combination of technical precision from nuclear operations and the adaptability required to master new programming languages and frameworks. I bring the same commitment to excellence that kept a nuclear facility running safely to every line of code I write.
